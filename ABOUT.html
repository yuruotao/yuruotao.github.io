Why build this website?

The idea originated when I was skimming through the passages and essays written over the years. I consider it a waste just leaving them to collect dust inside folders. Maybe in the future they could be of help to someone else. Plus, I like having a place to share my thoughts and ideas.

What contents are in this website?

There are 5 major pieces of content.

Shikemanping: the passages in this column are written in the name of Shike, a figure me and my mate Wang Yuwen created in 2020. We wanted to express our thoughts on social events.

Projects: the projects that I have developed as I learn. There are 3 major parts, Github projects, Science projects, and competitions.

Papers: here would contain the papers I publish in future years. I hope there would be a lot.

Learning: the notes and resources I found during my journey of study would be put here, I hope they may be of review value and helpful.

Curriculum Vitae: Document the stuff about me, including my education background, personal statement, ability and experience, awards and recommendations

Who is the author?

The name is Yu Ruotao, an undergraduate when writing this website. For more of my Information, please go and check out on my Curriculum Vitae. The link is here

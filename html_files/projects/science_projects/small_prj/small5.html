    In this essay, the author analyzed the control logic of an elevator. By separating into multiple function blocks, the author then write TIA portal programs accordingly. After that, the CPU model and surrounding functional devices are chosen. The basic functions of an elevator are initialization, calling, door-opening, floor number display, door control, and halt.
